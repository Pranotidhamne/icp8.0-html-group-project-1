Bharadwaj Sai Sudharsan (born 15 October 2001) is an Indian International cricketer, who plays for Tamil Nadu in domestic cricket and for Gujarat Titans in the IPL. He made his debut for Indian cricket team in One-day Internationals (ODIs) in a series against South Africa in December 2023.
