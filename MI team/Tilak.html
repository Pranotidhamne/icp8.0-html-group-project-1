Tilak Varma (born 8 November 2002) is an Indian International cricketer who plays as a left-handed batsman and part time off spin bowler. He made his International debut for India on 3 August 2023 against the West Indies. He plays for Hyderabad in domestic cricket and Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League(IPL). He was a part of the Indian squad which won the 2023 Asia Cup.
