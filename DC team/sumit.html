About

Kumar was selected to play for Jharkhand U-16 team. He made his Twenty20 debut on 3 January 2016 in the 2015–16 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy and went on to represent the same side for the next 2 years. He made his List A debut for Jharkhand in the 2017–18 Vijay Hazare Trophy on 5 February 2018.
