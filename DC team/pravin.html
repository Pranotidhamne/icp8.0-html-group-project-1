About

Pravin Dubey, born in Azamgarh, Uttar Pradesh, plays for Karnataka at the domestic level. A leg-spinner and a right-handed batsman, Dubey made his List A debut against Gujarat in the 2015 Vijay Hazare Trophy and has played one first-class match. Dubey has so far played 22 T20 games and 20 wickets against his name, with the best bowling figures of 4/19.
