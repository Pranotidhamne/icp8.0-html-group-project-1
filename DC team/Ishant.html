About

In May 2007, Sharma was selected in the Test squad to tour Bangladesh and replaced fast bowler Munaf Patel . There, in the second test, he bowled 3 overs, gave 5 runs and also took 1 wicket. After this, he was called for a tour of England in July-August 2007. Sharma was recalled to Team India for the third Test during Pakistan 's tour of India in December 2007 due to injuries to Zaheer Khan , RP Singh , and Shanthakumaran Sreesanth
