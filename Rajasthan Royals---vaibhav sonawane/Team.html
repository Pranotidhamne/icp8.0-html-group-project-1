OUR BRAND PURPOSE:

At Rajasthan Royals, we are on a mission to “Transform Society through Cricket, and Cricket through Innovation”. We are a global sports franchise that believes that royalty is not something one inherits, it is something that is to be earned. This 'Spirit of Royalty' is what sets Rajasthan Royals apart from the myriad other sports franchises globally. We call it being Royal at Heart.

So what exactly does it take to be a Royal at Heart? Winning in style. Never losing without a fight. Being resilient. Standing up - for yourself, the team and the game. Setting the bar high and leaving a legacy behind. But being large-hearted and welcoming at the same time.

All heart. All guts. That's what makes a Royal

It is this spirit that has led Rajasthan Royals to discover, nurture and promote new talent the future stars of Indian cricket. The Royals are also known for their innovative and pioneering approach.
The first player to win the Most Valuable Player award in an IPL and the one to win this award the maximum number of times in the history of the IPL was again a Royal - Shane Watson. We were also the first team to chase down a record IPL score of 217, a feat that stands unbroken to this day.
The Royals are rightly known as a team of firsts, and this extends to the digital realm as well - we were the first team to broadcast a designated YouTube Channel in November 2008 and to launch an electronic tablet in 2013.
